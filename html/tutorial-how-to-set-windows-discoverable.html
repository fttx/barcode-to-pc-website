How to make Windows discoverable

To make your PC discoverable, follow these steps:

1) Open the Wi-Fi settings

  • Press the Windows key on your keyboard. If your keyboard doesn't have a Windows key, press the Control (Ctrl) and Escape (Esc) keys on your keyboard.
  • Type "Change Wi-Fi settings" and press ENTER

2) Select "Manage known networks"

2) Open the network properties

2) Turn on the switch under Make this PC discoverable.
